Knowing the benefits of Drum therapy as an ancient way of promoting healing and self-expression through rhythms, our team aims to bring together people from all walks of life, create emotional harmony and help them cope with daily stress.

    Services

    "Strive for QUALITY not for QUANTITY"

    Corporate Drum Circle

    Our corporate session serves you the thaumaturgy of rhythm in your office space bringing you that much needed fresh breath of inspiration & energy, a perfect activity for staff motivation promoting bonding and mental wellness.

    Rhythm With Kids

    The best booster shot to spark their creativity & confidence. Our specially curated sessions for all age groups provide the little rockstars a chance to explore their inner musician, ranging from basic rhythms to playing styles.

    Wedding Drum Circle

    No wedding is complete without music, so why not do it with the thumpings of the drums & DW’s blazing energy ! Making your family & friends groove on our captivating beats. Sounds like an experience one would remember forever.

    Community Sessions (CSR)

    An open for all drumming platform giving exposure to every creator, visionary, professionals and hobbyists. Starting from kids to the elderlies, welcoming all age groups. Till now in our journey, we’ve had thousands of stories and countless beautiful smiles.

    Performance

    Definitely, you want the party to end at a high note, so we provide a variety of performances with Indian and international artists performing world music styles. From a percussion ensemble to a full band multi lingual, multi genre set up performing a set specific to client requirements.

    Learn & Join the Troupe

    Learning any Instrument without a teacher giving you regular feedbacks and resolving precisely the problem that you are facing is hard sometimes. So we at DrumWaves open our doors and welcome you to learn, master the art of world music and be a part of our amazing troupe.

    Rhythm With Junk

    One man’s junk is another man’s treasure and for us it’s a way of making music ! Yes, you heard it right. Building instruments & creating sound from plastic bottles, water drums, sticks etc.

    Leadership Training’s

    With over 10 years of collective facilitation experience and expertise we’re positive that we will be able to add energy, interaction, knowledge and synergy to your leadership training’s.

    Team building & Music workshops

    Workplace drumming & exciting musical workshops is an excellent team-building exercise that unites coworkers into a solid drumming band.
